                                         Communication
I'm not good at communication. Unless its about something I'm into I cant really keep a conversation going. I freeze sometimes. And its hard for me to look people in the eye. Though I am getting better, I still end up in awkward situations where I feel like I wanna shoot myself when talking to strangers. Shyness aside. Its the ---- I'm worried about. I lack experience. So I cant comfort well. I suck at apologizes. Asking for things is never my strong suit. I prefer to do things alone. But I'm trying. And I'm learning. I'm confident I can overcome my problem of communication within the next few years.

                                           Meticulous
The way I think is strange. I'm very strategic. Many people would consider that a good trait. In many ways it is. But for me, its annoying. A lot of the things I say or do are thought out and calculated. I lack the ability to act on instinct or impulse. "How will this benefit me?" "What will they think if I say this?" "If I say this right now then later I can use it as ----." When I say Hi its for a reason, when I say I love you its for a reason. When I tell her she's special its to make her love me more. Not because I feel that way but because it feels like I should. NO, I DO FEEL THAT WAY. But my thinking to much makes it feel less genuine. It makes me feel like a manipulator. My ulterior motives aren't meant to hurt the people I love. But what if they do at some point. Would that make me EVIL?

                                             Smile
I'm blessed with a big smile. This smile appears when I am complimented. This smile appears when I know I'm loved. This smile appears when I see other people happy. THIS same uncontrollable smile appears when I am scared. When I see terror. During grief. In times of despair. I smile in uncomfortable and awkward situations. Despite that I choose to look at it as a blessing rather than a curse. So imagine the pain I felt, when they told me they didn't love me, six different times, and not even a grin appeared.
